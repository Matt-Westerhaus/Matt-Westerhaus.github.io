Outdoors

I have always enjoyed playing sports. Growing up I played baseball, basketball, football, and ran track. Since starting college I have been playing a lot of spikeball in addition to playing on an intramural basketball team. I also joined a church league softball team where we won the championship 19-4. I like being outside in general so this is a good way for me to get outside, be active and be competative. One of my dreams is also to become a skydiver. I have only gone once so far but my goal, starting once I graduate, is to dive 25 times by the time I am 25 and to own my gear. I also love to camp.

Cards

As boring as they can sometimes sound, I really enjoy playing cards. My favorite casual games would have to be cribbage, euchre, durak, gin rummy, and solitare. I also enjoy the classics of war, kings in the corner, crazy eights, uno, you name it! I also really love going to the casino for blackjack and I recently learned how to play 3 card poker which has been fun.
